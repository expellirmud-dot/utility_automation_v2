# Governance Doctrine

## Governance First

Authority boundaries matter.

The system must preserve:
- auditability
- certification integrity
- authority separation
- deterministic governance

## AI Boundary
AI is advisory only.

AI cannot:
- self-promote authority
- rewrite governed truth
- bypass certification
- silently execute mutations

## Human / Governance Control
Mutation authority belongs to explicit governance mechanisms.

## Review Discipline
Claims require evidence.
Validation must be exact.
No fake "success" reporting.
